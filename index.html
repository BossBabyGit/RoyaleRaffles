Frequently Asked Questions

How do the raffles work?
Simply choose a raffle, select how many tickets you want to buy (¬£2 per ticket), and complete your payment. When the raffle ends, we draw the winner randomly and contact them immediately!
When do raffles end?
Raffles end either when all tickets are sold or when the time limit is reached, whichever comes first. You'll see a countdown timer on each raffle.
How do I know if I've won?
Winners are notified immediately via email and SMS. We also announce winners on our social media pages and winner gallery!
Can I choose cash instead of the prize?
Yes! Most of our prizes offer a cash alternative. You can choose when we contact you after winning.
Is there a free entry option?
Yes! UK law requires us to offer a free entry method. You can enter for free by answering a skill-based question and sending a postcard to our address.
How quickly are prizes delivered?
We aim to deliver all prizes within 7-14 days of the draw. Cars and high-value items may take slightly longer due to paperwork and logistics.
